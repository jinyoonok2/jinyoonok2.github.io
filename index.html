Jinyoon Kim

My research interests lie in Computer Vision. I am particularly interested in Object Recognition, Transformer Models, Interpretability, and the intersection between Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing, such as Video Language Models.

My goal is to research and refine the algorithms and applications of these technologies to benefit people across different societal bounds, from underdeveloped regions to urban cities.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Penn State University - Harrisburg.
