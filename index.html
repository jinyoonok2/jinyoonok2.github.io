Jinyoon Kim

My research interests lie in the broad fields of computer vision and machine learning, including Image Recognition, Medical Image Analysis, Object Detection, Object Perception, Deep Learning, and Reinforcement Learning. I am particularly interested in developing practical software solutions that enhance human life using emerging AI technologies.

Before graduating from PSU-Harrisburg in May 2024, I conducted research on image recognition under the supervision of Prof. Kabir. I also developed several software projects both through courseworks and extracurricular activities including Skin Cancer Detection Web Application, Face Recognition Program, and Machine Learning Classification on Mobile Application.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Penn State University - Harrisburg.
