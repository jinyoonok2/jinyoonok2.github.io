Introduction

I am in the process of completing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Penn State University, Harrisburg Campus.

Research Interests: Object Recognition, Image Segmentation (2D, 3D spatial), Transformers, Data Synthesis, Human Motion Capture, Video Language Models, and the intersection between Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing.

My research interests span various subjects within Artificial Intelligence, particularly in the field of Computer Vision, but also including Natural Language Processing Models. My goal is to research and refine the algorithms and applications of these technologies to benefit people across different societal bounds, from underdeveloped regions to urban cities.
