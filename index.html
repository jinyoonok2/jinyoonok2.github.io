About

Research Interests:

  • Object Recognition
  • Image Segmentation (2D, 3D spatial)
  • Transformers
  • Video Language Models
  • Egocentric Vision
  • Data Synthesis
  • Intersection between Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing
  • Human Motion Capture

I am in the process of completing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Penn State University - Harrisburg. My research interests span various subjects within Artificial Intelligence, particularly in the field of Computer Vision, but also including Natural Language Processing Models. My goal is to research and refine the algorithms and applications of these technologies to benefit people across different societal bounds, from underdeveloped regions to urban cities.
